## Manual station sequences

Operating sequences for Lines 13, 27, and 30 were curated from publicly available opening notices and encyclopedic station tables, then joined to OSM / Nominatim coordinates. Verify against the official Chengdu Rail map before relying on them operationally:

https://www.chengdurail.com/ckfw/xlt.htm
